Pacchi Pulusu Recipe

Pacchi Pulusu, also known as Rasam Pappu, is a tamarind based soup and made with red chillies and raw onions at the end to add the onion flavorful.

Ingredients and Instructions:

Ingredients Needed for Pacchi Pulusu:

  1. 3 cups of Soaked Tamarind water after hand pressing the tamrind and extracting the juice
  2. 2 table spoons of oil
  3. Salt to taste
  4. Cumins, and crushed red chilli peppers
  5. Can add Turmeric powder
  6. A piece of Jaggery optional tho
Step-by-Step Instructions

Prepare the Dal:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumins, and crushed red chilli peppers
  2. Add the tamarind water to the pan and bring it to a boil.
  3. Add salt, turmeric powder, and jaggery (if using) to the tamarind water. Stir well to dissolve the jaggery.
  4. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Once the pulusu has simmered, remove it from heat.
  6. Add the thinly sliced raw onions to the hot pulusu. The heat from the soup will slightly cook the onions while retaining their crunch and flavor.
  7. Give it a gentle stir to mix the onions into the pulusu.
  8. Serve hot as a soup or with steamed rice or can go well with muddha-pappu.
